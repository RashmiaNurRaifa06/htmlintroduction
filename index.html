Paris

Paris is the capital of the country of France. It has long been one of western Europe's major centers of culture and business. Some of the world's greatest artists, writers, scholars, fashion designers, and chefs have lived in Paris.Paris is known for its gorgeous, imposing monuments. These iconic structures, often an exemplar of a particular era in architecture, are one of the city's instantly recognizable elements. The preeminent of Paris's landmarks is the Eiffel Tower.
